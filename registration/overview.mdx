Alongside invoicing, TRONVoice includes a set of registration tools that help you keep accurate records for the Dutch tax authorities. Use them to log the kilometres you drive, manage your vehicles, and — when subcontractors are enabled — register worked hours.

You can find these features under **Rittenregistratie** (Trip registration) in the sidebar.

## What trip registration is for

The Dutch tax authorities require a complete and verifiable **kilometeradministratie** (mileage administration) when you use a vehicle for both business and private travel. For company cars, an accurate trip log is what lets you avoid or substantiate the *bijtelling* (taxable benefit). For private cars used for business, a trip log substantiates the €0.23 per kilometre allowance you can declare.

TRONVoice's trip registration keeps a per-trip record of the date, route, distance, and whether each trip was business (**zakelijk**) or private (**privé**). You can export the full log at any time as a spreadsheet for your accountant or for your tax records.

### Vehicles (Voertuigen)

Every trip is linked to a **voertuig** (vehicle). Before you can log trips, register at least one vehicle. TRONVoice looks up the vehicle's details from the **RDW** (the Dutch vehicle authority) using its **kenteken** (license plate), so you only need to type the plate.

A vehicle is marked as either **zakelijk** (business) or **privé** (private). This determines how trips for that vehicle are logged — business vehicles track odometer readings, while private vehicles record distance directly. See [Managing vehicles](/registration/vehicles).

### Trips (Ritten)

A **rit** (trip) is a single journey from a departure address to an arrival address on a given date. Each trip records the distance driven and is classified as business or private. You can also link a trip to a client and add a description of its purpose. See [Logging trips](/registration/trips).

### Favourite routes (Favoriete routes)

If you regularly drive the same journey, save it as a **favoriete route** (favourite route). A favourite route stores the departure and arrival addresses, distance, trip type, and optionally a client. From then on you can create a fully pre-filled trip from that route with a single click. See [Favourite routes](/registration/trips#favourite-routes).

## What hours registration is for

**Urenregistratie** (Hours registration) is a separate tool for businesses that work with subcontractors. It lets you log the hours your subcontractors worked each week in a weekly grid, approve them, and turn approved hours into invoices automatically.

<Note>
  Hours registration is only available when **subcontractors** (onderaannemers) are enabled for your organisation. If you do not see a **Uren** (Hours) item in the navigation, this feature is not active for your account.
</Note>

See [Hours registration](/registration/hours) for the full workflow.

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your vehicles">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Log your trips">
    Record each journey as you drive it, or in batches. [Save favourite routes](/registration/trips#favourite-routes) for journeys you repeat.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export for your accountant">
    At the end of a period, [export the trip log](/registration/trips#exporting-your-trip-log) as a spreadsheet for your records.
  </Step>
</Steps>
