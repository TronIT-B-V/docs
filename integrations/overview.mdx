TRONVoice connects to the external services you already use, so your invoicing flows straight into your payments, accounting, archive, and inbox without manual work. Every connection is managed from a single **Integraties** (Integrations) screen.

<Note>
  Integrations are configured **per administration**. If you have multiple administrations, set up each connection separately for each one.
</Note>

## Open the Integraties screen

Go to **Settings → Integrations**. If you have more than one administration, choose the one you want to configure from the selector at the top. With a single administration, it is selected automatically.

The screen groups integrations into tabs:

- **Betalingen** (Payments) — collect invoice payments online.
- **Boekhouding** (Accounting) — sync invoices and clients to your accounting package.
- **Opslag** (Storage) — archive invoice PDFs in your own cloud storage.
- **Communicatie** (Communication) — send invoices from your own email domain.

Use the **Alle** (All) tab to see every integration at once.

## Payments

Choose how clients pay your invoices. An administration uses **one** payment provider at a time — activating one switches the other off.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Mollie" icon="credit-card" href="/payments/mollie">
    Connect a Mollie account to add an online payment link to every invoice. Clients pay via iDEAL, credit card, Bancontact, and more, and invoices are marked paid automatically via webhook.
  </Card>
  <Card title="bunq.me" icon="link" href="/payments/bunq">
    Add a free bunq.me payment link to invoices. Clients pay via iDEAL with no transaction fees and the money lands straight in your bunq account.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Accounting

You can connect **one** accounting package per administration.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Exact Online" icon="book-open" href="/integrations/exact-online">
    Push sent invoices to Exact Online as sales entries, receive payment status updates, and import your debtors as clients.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Minox" icon="calculator" href="/integrations/minox">
    Connect the Minox accounting package via OAuth, sync invoices, and import your clients into TRONVoice.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SnelStart — coming soon" icon="clock">
    A SnelStart accounting integration is on the roadmap. It is not available yet.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  Exact Online and Minox cannot be active at the same time for the same administration. Disconnect the existing accounting integration before activating another one.
</Warning>

Once an accounting integration is active, invoices that reach the **Sent** status are exported automatically, and payment statuses flow back into TRONVoice. You can also import your existing clients from the accounting package so both systems stay aligned.

## Email

By default, TRONVoice sends invoice and quote emails from a TRONVoice address. With a custom email domain, messages are sent from your own address instead.

</CardGroup>

## How integration cards work

Each integration appears as a card on its tab. The card shows the current status — connected, not connected, or needs attention — and the actions available for that service.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the card">
    Click the card for the service you want to set up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect or configure">
    Depending on the integration, you either start a secure connection (OAuth, for Exact Online, Minox, and Ponto), enter connection details (for SharePoint), or fill in a setting (for bunq.me).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Once saved, the integration runs automatically in the background for that administration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Set up your integrations right after creating an administration. They start working immediately for every invoice you send afterwards.
</Tip>
