title: "Users and roles: invite and manage your team"
description: "Invite team members to TRONVoice, assign them a role, and manage access. Invitations expire after 7 days and all users must set up 2FA."
---

You can give other people access to your TRONVoice account by inviting them by email. Each user is assigned a role that determines what they can do. Manage your team under **Settings → Users**.

## Roles

There are three roles in TRONVoice:

| Role | What they can do |
|---|---|
| **Member** (Lid) | Create and manage clients, invoices, and quotes in the administrations they have access to |
| **Administrator** (Beheerder) | Everything a member can do, plus invite and manage other users |
| **Owner** (Eigenaar) | Full access, including organisation-level settings, billing, and administration management |

<Note>
  Organisation settings — such as adding administrations, managing billing, and configuring subscriptions — are only available to owners.
</Note>

## Invite a team member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open users">
    Go to **Settings → Users** and click **New user**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their details">
    Fill in the new user's **name** and **email address**. The invitation will be sent to the email address you enter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign a role">
    Select a role:
    - **Lid** — for team members who create and send invoices
    - **Beheerder** — for team leads or office managers who also need to manage users
    - **Eigenaar** — for co-owners who need full access
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invitation">
    Click **Uitnodiging versturen**. TRONVoice sends an invitation email to the address you entered.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**What happens next:**

1. The invited user receives an email with a personal invitation link.
2. They click the link and choose a password.
3. They are prompted to set up two-factor authentication (2FA).
4. Once 2FA is configured, they have access to the account.

<Warning>
  Invitation links expire after **7 days**. If the user does not accept within that time, you will need to resend the invitation.
</Warning>

## Resend an invitation

If an invitation has expired or the user did not receive the email:

1. Go to **Settings → Users**.
2. Find the user with a pending invite (shown with an "Uitgenodigd" badge).
3. Click the resend icon next to their name.

A new invitation email is sent immediately, and the 7-day expiry resets.

## User status

Each user in the list shows one of the following statuses:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Uitgenodigd** | Invitation sent, not yet accepted |
| **Actief** | Account active with two-factor authentication enabled |
| **Geen 2FA** | Account active but two-factor authentication not yet set up |

## Remove a user

1. Go to **Settings → Users**.
2. Click the delete icon next to the user you want to remove.
3. Confirm the deletion.

<Warning>
  You cannot delete your own account from this screen. To close your own account, contact TRONVoice support.
</Warning>

Removing a user immediately revokes their access. Any invoices, quotes, or clients they created remain in the account and are not affected.

## Two-factor authentication

All users are required to set up two-factor authentication (2FA) before they can access the account. This happens automatically as part of the invitation flow. If a user skips 2FA setup, they will be prompted to complete it on their next login.
