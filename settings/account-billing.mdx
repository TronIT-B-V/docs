Your TRONVoice **account billing** covers the platform itself — the optional paid features you add to your account. This is separate from [credits](/settings/credits), which pay for sending individual documents.

There are two platform subscriptions:

- **Administrations** — paying for extra administrations beyond the 2 free ones.
- **Bank connections** — a paid add-on for connecting your bank accounts.

Both are collected by SEPA direct debit, and TRONVoice generates an invoice for every charge.

## Administrations subscription

Every account includes **2 administrations at no cost**. Each additional administration costs **€3.00 per month**.

Adding one or more paid administrations also gives you a **10% discount on sending costs** across your entire account. See [Credits](/settings/credits) for how that discount applies.

### Change the number of administrations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open subscription management">
    Go to **Abonnement → Beheer** (Subscription → Manage).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the number of administrations">
    Use the slider to choose how many administrations you need, from 2 up to 20.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up payment if needed">
    If you go beyond the 2 free administrations and do not yet have a payment method, you are taken to checkout to set up a SEPA mandate first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Your new limit is applied and your monthly cost is shown. The extra administrations become available immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  For more than 20 administrations, contact the sales team at [sales@tronvoice.eu](mailto:sales@tronvoice.eu).
</Note>

### Cancel the administrations subscription

Go to **Abonnement → Beheer** and choose to cancel. Your account is downgraded to the 2 free administrations and the recurring charge stops.

<Warning>
  Cancelling does not delete data. All invoices, clients, and documents in your administrations remain accessible. Make sure you are within the free limit of 2 active administrations before cancelling.
</Warning>

## Bank connections subscription

Connecting a bank account to TRONVoice for automatic transaction import is a paid add-on. There is no free tier — each bank connection costs **€3.99 per month**.

### Add bank connections

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open bank subscription management">
    Go to **Bank-abonnement → Beheer** (Bank subscription → Manage).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the number of connections">
    Use the slider to select how many bank connections you need, up to 10.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up payment">
    If you do not yet have a payment method, you are taken to checkout to set up a SEPA mandate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your bank">
    After activation, you are taken to a page where you can connect your first bank account right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  For more than 10 bank connections, contact the sales team at [sales@tronvoice.eu](mailto:sales@tronvoice.eu).
</Note>

### Cancel the bank subscription

Go to **Bank-abonnement → Beheer** and choose to cancel. Cancelling **removes all active bank connections** and their imported transactions, and stops the recurring charge.

<Warning>
  Cancelling the bank subscription deletes your bank connections and their imported transactions. Export anything you need first.
</Warning>

## How platform billing is collected

Both subscriptions are paid by **SEPA direct debit** via Mollie.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up a mandate">
    The first time you take out a paid subscription, you complete a payment via Mollie that establishes a SEPA mandate authorizing TRONVoice to collect by direct debit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Recurring collection">
    Each month, TRONVoice collects the amount due for your active subscriptions automatically. Your subscription period extends with every successful payment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  If a recurring payment fails, TRONVoice notifies the account owner so you can resolve it. Keep enough balance on the linked bank account to avoid interruptions.
</Info>

## Download your TRONVoice invoices

TRONVoice generates an invoice for every platform charge — credit top-ups, the administrations subscription, and the bank connections subscription. These are your **TRONVoice platform invoices**.

To view them, go to **Profile → Facturen** (Invoices).

Each invoice shows its number, date, type, and total amount.

### Download a single invoice

Click the download action next to any invoice to download its PDF.

### Download multiple invoices

To download several at once, select the invoices you want and use the bulk download action.

- Selecting **one** invoice downloads that PDF directly.
- Selecting **multiple** invoices downloads them together as a single ZIP file named `TRONVoice-Facturen-<date>.zip`.

<Tip>
  Platform invoices are created automatically — there is nothing to request. They appear under **Profile → Facturen** as soon as a payment is confirmed.
</Tip>
