TRONVoice uses a pay-as-you-go **credit** balance to cover the cost of sending documents. Every invoice or quote you send by email deducts a small amount from your credit balance. There is no monthly subscription for sending — you only pay for what you send.

<Note>
  New accounts receive **€2.50 in free credits** as a welcome bonus to get you started.
</Note>

## What sending costs

Each sent invoice or quote costs **€0.65** in credits.

If you have one or more paid extra administrations, you receive a **10% discount** on this cost across your entire account, bringing it to **€0.585** per document. See [Account billing](/settings/account-billing) for how to add extra administrations.

<Info>
  Receiving payments, creating drafts, generating PDFs, and previewing documents are always free. Credits are only deducted when a document is actually sent.
</Info>

## The monthly cap

TRONVoice applies a **monthly cap of €16.25** on sending costs. Once your deductions reach the cap within a billing period, every further invoice and quote is sent **for free** until the period resets.

Your billing period runs from your account's registration day each month to the day before the next month's registration day. The Credits page shows your current period, how much you have spent, and how much allowance remains.

<Tip>
  If you send documents frequently, you will often hit the cap early in the month — making the effective cost per document much lower than €0.65.
</Tip>

## View your balance and transactions

Your credit balance is shown in the top bar of the dashboard. For full detail, go to **Credits**.

The Credits page shows:

- Your current **credit balance**.
- The **monthly cap** progress for the current billing period.
- A complete **transaction history**.

Each transaction has a type:

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| Opwaardering | A top-up that added credits to your balance |
| Afschrijving | A deduction for a sent invoice or quote |
| Bonus | The welcome bonus or credits granted manually |
| Correctie | A correction, for example after a failed SEPA payment |

Transactions linked to a specific invoice or quote reference that document, and each row shows your balance after the transaction.

## Top up your credits

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the top-up page">
    Go to **Credits** and click **Opwaarderen** (Top up).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an amount">
    Select one of the preset amounts — **€5, €10, €25, €50, €100, or €250** — or enter your own. The minimum top-up is **€5.00**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay via Mollie">
    You are redirected to Mollie to complete the payment with your preferred method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Credits are added">
    Once the payment is confirmed, the credits are added to your balance and a TRONVoice invoice is generated for the top-up.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Most payment methods confirm instantly. If your payment is still processing, your credits appear as soon as it is confirmed — this can take a few minutes.
</Note>

## Set up auto top-up

Auto top-up keeps you from ever being blocked from sending. When your balance falls below a threshold you set, TRONVoice automatically charges your bank account via SEPA direct debit and grants the credits immediately.

### Set up a SEPA mandate

Auto top-up requires a SEPA direct debit mandate.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the mandate">
    On the **Credits** page, start the SEPA mandate setup. You are redirected to Mollie.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the mandate">
    Complete a small verification payment to authorize TRONVoice to collect by direct debit. After returning, your mandate is active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Configure auto top-up

Once your mandate is active, enable auto top-up and set:

- **Drempelbedrag** (threshold) — when your balance falls below this amount, a top-up is triggered. Between **€1.00** and **€50.00**.
- **Opwaardeerbedrag** (top-up amount) — how much is added each time. Between **€10.00** and **€250.00**.

Save your settings to activate auto top-up.

<Info>
  SEPA direct debit takes 2–5 business days to confirm, so TRONVoice grants auto top-up credits **immediately** when the payment is started — you never have to wait. If the direct debit later fails, those credits are deducted again and a correction is recorded.
</Info>

## When credits run out

If you try to send an invoice or quote without enough credits, TRONVoice shows the **insufficient credits** screen instead of sending.

From there you can top up directly or set up auto top-up. As soon as your balance is sufficient, you can send the document.

<Tip>
  With auto top-up enabled, TRONVoice tops up automatically the moment you run short — so you are not interrupted at all.
</Tip>

Note that if you have already reached the **monthly cap**, documents are sent for free and a low balance does not block you.
