TRONVoice tracks every invoice from the moment you create it until it is fully paid. This page explains the status lifecycle, how to record payments, how partial payments work, and how clients view an invoice through a secure public link.

## Invoice status lifecycle

Every invoice moves through a clear set of statuses.

```
Concept → Verzonden → Betaald
                    → Gecrediteerd
```

| Status | Dutch label | What it means |
|---|---|---|
| **Draft** | **Concept** | The invoice is being prepared. You can still edit every detail. It has a temporary `CONCEPT-XXXXX` number. |
| **Sent** | **Verzonden** | The invoice has been sent or marked as sent. A permanent invoice number is assigned and the payment deadline starts. |
| **Overdue** | **Vervallen** | A sent invoice whose due date (**vervaldatum**) has passed without full payment. This is a display state of **Verzonden** — not a separate stored status. |
| **Partially paid** | **Deelbetaald** | A sent invoice that has received some, but not all, of its total amount through matched bank transactions. |
| **Paid** | **Betaald** | The full amount has been received and confirmed. |
| **Credited** | **Gecrediteerd** | A credit note has been issued against the invoice. It can no longer be paid. |

<Note>
**Vervallen** and **Deelbetaald** are not stored statuses — they are derived from a **Verzonden** invoice based on its due date and matched payments. The invoice list shows them as distinct badges so you can see at a glance what needs attention.
</Note>

## Mark an invoice as paid

When you receive a payment outside of an automatic channel — for example, a bank transfer you spotted manually — you can mark the invoice as paid.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invoice">
    Go to **Invoices** and open the sent invoice you received payment for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark as paid">
    Click **Mark as paid** (**Markeer als betaald**). A dialog appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the payment details">
    Optionally set:
    - **Payment date** (**Betaald op**) — defaults to today if left blank.
    - **Payment reference** (**Betaal referentie**) — a free-text note, such as a bank statement line number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm the dialog. The status changes to **Betaald**, the payment method is recorded as **Handmatig** (Manual), and the change is logged in the invoice activity log.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If your administration has payment confirmation emails enabled, TRONVoice sends a confirmation message to the client when the invoice is marked as paid.

<Tip>
Marked the wrong invoice as paid? Open it and change the status back to **Verzonden**. TRONVoice clears the payment date, method, and reference so the invoice returns to its open state.
</Tip>

## How payments link to bank transactions

When you connect a bank feed, TRONVoice matches incoming transactions to your sent invoices.

- A matched transaction records a **matched amount** (**matched bedrag**) against the invoice.
- An invoice can be matched to **multiple transactions** — which is what makes partial payments possible.
- When the total matched amount covers the full invoice total, the invoice is considered fully paid.

The invoice detail page shows every matched bank transaction under the **Payment** section, including the date and matched amount.

## Partial payments

A **partial payment** (**deelbetaald**) is a sent invoice that has received some payment but is not yet fully covered.

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Total paid** | The sum of all matched bank transaction amounts. |
| **Outstanding amount** | The invoice total minus the total paid. |
| **Payment progress** | The paid amount as a percentage of the invoice total. |

A partially paid invoice keeps the **Verzonden** status but shows a **Deelbetaald** badge in the invoice list. It only becomes **Betaald** once the outstanding amount reaches zero (within a one-cent tolerance).

<Note>
Partial payments are driven entirely by matched bank transactions. Marking an invoice as paid manually sets it straight to **Betaald** for the full amount — it does not record a partial amount.
</Note>

## Online payments (iDEAL / bunq)

If your administration has Mollie (iDEAL) or bunq configured, sent invoices can be paid online directly from the invoice email or the public invoice page.

When a client pays online:
- The invoice is automatically marked as **Betaald**.
- The payment method is recorded as **iDEAL (Mollie)** or **bunq.me**.
- A payment confirmation email is sent to the client.

Online payment is only available for sent invoices with a positive amount that have not been credited.

## The public invoice view

Every invoice has a unique, token-based public page that your client can open without a TRONVoice account.

- The URL contains a secure **payment token** unique to that invoice.
- The page shows the full invoice details and lets the client view and download the PDF.
- If online payment is available, a payment button appears on the page.
- Each visit and PDF download is logged in the invoice activity log, so you can see when a client viewed it.

<Tip>
You can copy the public invoice link from the invoice detail page and share it manually — for example, by WhatsApp — if a client says they never received the email.
</Tip>

<Warning>
Anyone with the link can view the invoice. Treat the public URL like a password and only share it with the intended recipient.
</Warning>
