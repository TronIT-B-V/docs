---
title: "Send an invoice to your client"
description: "How to send a TRONVoice invoice by email, schedule it for a future date, use bulk send, share a payment link, or mark it as sent manually."
---

Once you have finished editing a draft invoice, you can send it to your client. TRONVoice sends the invoice as a PDF attachment by email, assigns the final invoice number, and starts tracking the payment deadline from that moment.

<Note>
Before you can send an invoice, your administration must have an IBAN configured. If the IBAN is missing, TRONVoice will show an error and direct you to the administration settings.
</Note>

## Send immediately

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invoice">
    Go to **Invoices**, find the draft invoice you want to send, and open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send">
    Click the **Send** button. A confirmation dialog appears where you can choose:
    - **Send to client** — sends the invoice to the client's email address.
    - **Send to admin** — sends a BCC copy to the email address configured for your administration.

    Both options are enabled by default. You can turn either off if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Send invoice**. TRONVoice queues the email and you are returned to the invoice detail page. The invoice status changes to **Sent**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The email sent to your client uses the **Invoice to client** email template. You can customize the subject line and body text under **Settings → Email templates**.

A **€0.65 credit** is deducted from your account balance when the invoice is sent. If you have reached the monthly cap of €16.25, the invoice is sent for free.

## Schedule sending for a later date

You can set an invoice to send automatically at a future date and time — useful for month-end billing or when you want to prepare invoices in advance.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the draft invoice">
    Go to **Invoices** and open the draft you want to schedule.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open schedule options">
    Click **Schedule** (or the calendar icon next to the Send button). A date and time picker appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the date and time">
    Choose a date (today or later) and a time in 15-minute intervals. The date and time must be in the future.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose recipients">
    Select whether to send to the client, the admin, or both.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Schedule**. The invoice detail page shows the planned send date. The invoice remains a draft until that moment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To cancel a scheduled send, open the invoice and click **Cancel schedule**.

## Bulk send

You can send multiple draft invoices at once from the invoice list.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select invoices">
    On the **Invoices** list, tick the checkboxes next to the draft invoices you want to send.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open bulk actions">
    Click **Bulk actions** and choose **Send selected invoices**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose recipients and confirm">
    Select whether to send to clients, admins, or both. Click **Send**. TRONVoice processes each invoice in sequence.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Invoices are skipped (with a warning) if:
- The administration has no IBAN configured.
- Today is an excluded invoicing day for the client.
- You have insufficient credits for the number of invoices being sent.

## Resend an invoice

If a client has not received the email or has lost it, you can resend a sent invoice without changing its status or number.

Open the invoice and click **Resend**. The send dialog appears with the same recipient options. Resending does not deduct additional credits.

You can also bulk resend multiple sent invoices from the invoice list using **Bulk actions → Resend selected**.

## Payment link

When your administration has Mollie (iDEAL) configured, every sent invoice includes a **payment link** in the email. Clients can click this link to pay directly online. The link opens a secure, public payment page showing the invoice details.

The payment URL is token-based and unique to each invoice. It does not require your client to have a TRONVoice account.

When a client pays via iDEAL, the invoice is automatically marked as **Paid** and a payment confirmation email is sent to the client.

<Tip>
If you want to share the payment link manually (for example, via WhatsApp), you can copy it from the invoice detail page under the **Payment** section.
</Tip>

If bunq is configured instead of Mollie, the payment link points to a bunq.me page pre-filled with the invoice amount and number.

## Mark as sent (without email)

If you have already sent the invoice outside of TRONVoice — for example, by post or through another system — you can mark it as sent manually. This assigns the final invoice number and starts the payment deadline without sending any email.

Open the draft invoice and click **Mark as sent**. The status changes to **Sent** and a credit is deducted from your balance.

<Warning>
You cannot undo "Mark as sent." If you need to make changes after marking an invoice as sent, you must either use the edit function (if enabled for your administration) or issue a credit note.
</Warning>
