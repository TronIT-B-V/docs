---
title: "Create an invoice in TRONVoice"
description: "Step-by-step guide to creating an invoice in TRONVoice: select a client, add line items, configure VAT options, apply discounts, and save."
---

Creating an invoice in TRONVoice takes only a few steps. Invoices start as drafts, so you can take your time filling in the details before sending.

## Create a new invoice

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invoice creator">
    Go to **Invoices** in the sidebar and click **New invoice**. If you have more than one administration, select the relevant one from the dropdown. If you only have one, it is selected automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a client">
    Choose the client from the **Client** dropdown. Only active clients for the selected administration are shown.

    If the client does not exist yet, use the **+ New client** option to create one inline without leaving the form.

    <Tip>
      When you select a client, TRONVoice automatically applies that client's VAT settings. For example, if the client is configured for reverse charge (BTW verlegd), the VAT fields update automatically.
    </Tip>

    TRONVoice can also pre-fill the invoice lines from your most recent invoice for this client. You can enable or disable this under your administration settings (**Reuse invoice lines**).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set the invoice date and payment term">
    The **Invoice date** defaults to today. The **Payment term** (in days) determines the due date automatically. The default payment term is taken from your administration settings (typically 30 days).

    You can also set a **Reference** — a free-text field that appears on the invoice, useful for purchase order numbers or project codes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add line items">
    Click **Add line** to add a product or service to the invoice.

    For each line:
    - Select a product from your catalogue or type a description manually.
    - Enter the **quantity** and **unit price**.
    - Optionally set a **unit** (e.g. hours, pieces).
    - Optionally group the line under a **location** (useful for project-based invoicing).

    You can also add **note rows** — text-only lines without pricing, useful for remarks or section headings.

    Lines can be reordered by dragging them, or moved up and down using the arrow buttons.

    <Tip>
      You can enter prices inclusive of VAT by toggling the **Prices include VAT** option. TRONVoice converts the price to the excl. VAT amount automatically when saving the line.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure VAT (BTW)">
    VAT is set per line item. For each line you can choose:

    | Option | When to use |
    |---|---|
    | **Standard rate** (21%, 9%, or 0%) | Default for most products and services sold in the Netherlands. |
    | **Reverse charge (BTW verlegd)** | For services provided to VAT-registered businesses in other EU countries. VAT is set to 0% and the invoice states that VAT has been reversed. |
    | **VAT exempt (BTW vrijgesteld)** | For services that are legally exempt from VAT, such as certain medical or educational services. |

    <Note>
      When you select reverse charge or VAT exempt for a line, the VAT rate is automatically set to 0% and the other option is deselected.
    </Note>

    If your client is configured as a reverse-charge or exempt client, all new lines will default to that setting automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Apply a discount (optional)">
    To apply a discount to the entire invoice, enter a **Discount percentage** in the invoice header. This discount is applied to the subtotal before VAT is calculated.

    For line-level discounts, enter a percentage in the **Discount** field on an individual line.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add attachments (optional)">
    You can attach files to the invoice — for example, a timesheet, delivery note, or signed contract. Supported formats: PDF, Word, Excel, images (JPG, PNG), and plain text files. Maximum file size is 10 MB per file.

    Enable **Merge PDFs into invoice** to combine all PDF attachments into a single PDF document when downloading or sending the invoice.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the draft">
    Click **Save invoice** to create the invoice as a draft. You can come back and edit it at any time before sending.

    A temporary draft number (`CONCEPT-XXXXX`) is assigned. The permanent invoice number is only assigned when the invoice is sent.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Invoice number format

When an invoice is sent, TRONVoice assigns the next invoice number in sequence. The format depends on your administration settings:

- You set a **prefix** for your administration (e.g. `F-` or `INV-`).
- You can include `[jaar]` (year) and `[maand]` (month) as placeholders in the prefix — for example, `F-[jaar]-` produces `F-2025-00001`.
- The sequence resets at the start of each year.

You can configure the prefix in **Settings → Administration → Invoice settings**.

## UBL / e-invoice format

If your administration has UBL export enabled, a **UBL** button appears on the invoice detail page. This downloads the invoice as a UBL 2.1 XML file — a structured electronic invoice format accepted by most Dutch and European accounting packages.

<Note>
UBL export must be enabled per administration. Contact your administrator or check **Settings → Administration → Invoice settings**.
</Note>

## Historical invoice import

If you are migrating from another system, you can import existing invoices as historical records. Use the **Import existing invoice** option on the invoice creator. You can set the original invoice number and choose whether to import it as already sent or already paid.

Historical invoices do not go through the normal draft → sent flow and do not consume credits.
