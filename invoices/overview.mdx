Invoices are the core of TRONVoice. You create them as drafts, send them to your clients, and track payment — all in one place. This page explains how invoices work, what statuses they can have, and what tools are available to you.

## Invoice lifecycle

Every invoice in TRONVoice follows a clear path from creation to payment.

```
Draft (Concept) → Sent (Verzonden) → Paid (Betaald)
                                   → Credited (Gecrediteerd)
```

| Status | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Draft** | The invoice is being prepared. You can still edit all details and line items. No invoice number has been assigned yet. |
| **Sent** | The invoice has been sent to your client or manually marked as sent. A real invoice number is assigned at this point. The client's payment deadline is tracked from here. |
| **Paid** | You or TRONVoice has confirmed payment — either automatically via iDEAL/Mollie, matched from your bank feed, or marked manually. |
| **Credited** | A credit note has been issued against this invoice. The original invoice is locked and can no longer be paid. |

<Note>
Once an invoice moves from Draft to Sent, it receives its permanent invoice number. Draft invoices use a temporary `CONCEPT-XXXXX` number. You can configure the number format (prefix, year, sequence) in your administration settings.
</Note>

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="PDF & UBL export" icon="download">
    Download any invoice as a PDF or as a UBL 2.1 XML e-invoice — compatible with most accounting software.

  <Card title="VAT options (BTW)" icon="percent">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Discounts" icon="tag">
    Add a percentage or fixed-amount discount at the invoice level, or apply line-level discounts to individual items.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Attachments" icon="paperclip">
    Upload PDF files as attachments. Optionally merge them into the invoice PDF for a single combined document.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Scheduled sending" icon="clock">
    Set a future date and time to send an invoice automatically. Useful for month-end billing cycles.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Online payment link" icon="credit-card">
    Share a secure payment link with your client so they can pay via iDEAL directly from the invoice email.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automated reminders" icon="bell">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Credit notes" icon="file-minus">
    Issue a credit note directly from a sent invoice to correct or reverse a charge.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bulk actions" icon="list">
    Send, mark as paid, download, or delete multiple invoices at once from the invoice list.

## Credit pricing

Each invoice you send costs **€0.65 in credits**. Credits are deducted from your account balance at the moment an invoice is sent or manually marked as sent.

<Note>
There is a monthly cap of **€16.25**. Once you reach that amount in a billing period, all further invoices are free for the remainder of the month.
</Note>

If your credit balance is insufficient when you try to send an invoice, TRONVoice will redirect you to the credit top-up page before proceeding.

Credit notes do not cost credits to send.
