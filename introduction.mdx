TRONVoice is an online invoicing platform built specifically for Dutch businesses. Whether you're a freelancer (ZZP'er), a small business, or an accountant managing multiple clients, TRONVoice lets you create, send, and track invoices without a fixed monthly subscription. You pay only for what you send.

## How pricing works

TRONVoice uses a pay-as-you-go credit model — there is no fixed monthly fee just for sending invoices.

| What | Cost |
|------|------|
| Sending an invoice or quote | €0.65 per document |
| Monthly cap | €16.25 |
| After the cap | Free for the rest of the month |
| 2 administrations | Included for free |
| Each additional administration | €3.00 / month |

Once you reach the monthly cap of €16.25, all further invoices and quotes that month cost nothing. This makes TRONVoice cost-effective whether you send 5 invoices a month or 500.

<Tip>
  If you add extra administrations (beyond the 2 included), you also receive a 10% discount on invoice costs, bringing the per-invoice price down to €0.59.
</Tip>

## Multi-administration support

One TRONVoice account can manage multiple separate business entities, called **administraties**. Each administration has its own invoice series, logo, VAT settings, and client list. This is ideal for:

- Holding companies with multiple operating entities
- Accountants or bookkeepers managing client books
- Business owners with more than one company

Your account includes 2 administrations at no extra cost. You can add more at €3.00 per administration per month.

## BTW (VAT) compliance

<Note>
  TRONVoice is built for the Dutch tax system. Invoices automatically include the correct BTW (VAT) rate (21% standard, or 9% / 0% where applicable). Your BTW number appears on every invoice, and the platform supports BTW-verlegd (reverse charge) for transactions with other VAT-registered businesses.
</Note>
