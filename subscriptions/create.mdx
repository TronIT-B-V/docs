---
title: "Create a recurring invoice"
description: "Set up a subscription so TRONVoice generates invoices for a client automatically on a fixed schedule."
---

A subscription is a recurring invoice template tied to a client. You define the invoice lines and a billing schedule once, and TRONVoice generates a new invoice every time the schedule comes due.

## Create a subscription

Go to **Subscriptions** in the main navigation and click **New subscription**. You can also start one from a client record, which pre-selects the client and administration.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the administration and client">
    Choose the **administration** the subscription belongs to, then the **client** (**klant**). Only active clients in that administration are listed.

    When you pick a client, TRONVoice applies their VAT situation automatically — **reversed VAT** (**BTW verlegd**) for EU clients, **VAT exempt** (**BTW vrijgesteld**) for non-EU clients, or the client's default VAT percentage otherwise.

    <Tip>
      If your organisation has a single administration, it's selected for you.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set the basic details">
    | Field | Notes |
    |---|---|
    | **Name** (**Naam**) | Internal name for the subscription, e.g. `Monthly hosting`. Required. |
    | **Reference** (**Referentie**) | Optional reference shown on generated invoices. |
    | **Billing period label** (**Facturatie periode**) | Optional free-text label such as `December 2024`. |
    | **Notes** (**Notities**) | Optional internal notes. |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose the billing interval">
    The interval has two parts: an **interval type** and an **interval value**. The value multiplies the type — set the value to `2` with type **Week** for an invoice every two weeks.

    | Interval type | One invoice every… |
    |---|---|
    | **Day** (**Dag**) | day |
    | **Week** | week |
    | **Month** (**Maand**) | month |
    | **Quarter** (**Kwartaal**) | three months |
    | **Year** (**Jaar**) | year |
    | **2 years** (**2 Jaar**) | two years |

    The interval value can be any whole number from 1 to 99.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set the dates">
    | Field | Notes |
    |---|---|
    | **Start date** (**Startdatum**) | When the subscription begins. Defaults to today. |
    | **First invoice date** (**Volgende factuurdatum**) | The date the first invoice is generated. Defaults to today. |
    | **End date** (**Einddatum**) | Optional. Leave empty for an open-ended subscription. Must be after the start date. |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add invoice lines">
    Add one or more lines that will appear on every generated invoice. Each line can be entered manually or filled from a product in your catalogue. For each line you can set:

    - **Description** (**Omschrijving**)
    - **Quantity and unit** (**Aantal** / **Eenheid**)
    - **Unit price** (**Eenheidsprijs**)
    - **VAT rate** (**BTW**) — per line: a percentage, **reversed**, or **exempt**
    - **Discount** (**Korting**) — a percentage off that line
    - **Location group** (**Locatiegroep**) — to group lines under a header

    You can also add **note lines** (**notitieregels**) — text-only lines with no price. Lines can be reordered by dragging.

    <Note>
      Line descriptions support date variables. When an invoice is generated, placeholders are replaced with the current period — handy for descriptions like "Hosting [maand] [jaar]".
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set an overall discount (optional)">
    Enter a **discount percentage** (**Korting percentage**) to apply to the subtotal of every generated invoice, on top of any per-line discounts.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure sending options">
    | Option | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Active** (**Actief**) | Inactive subscriptions never generate invoices. On by default. |
    | **Auto-send** (**Auto verzenden**) | Email the generated invoice automatically. Off by default. |
    | **Send to administration** (**Verzend naar admin**) | Send a copy to your own administration email. |
    | **Send to client** (**Verzend naar klant**) | Send the invoice to the client's email address. |

    If auto-send is off, generated invoices are saved as drafts for you to review and send manually.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the subscription">
    Click **Save**. The subscription opens its detail page and will generate its first invoice on the first invoice date you set.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How invoices get generated

When a subscription's **first invoice date** is today or in the past, the subscription is **due**. Generating an invoice — automatically, manually, or in bulk — does the following:

1. Creates an invoice in the subscription's administration for its client, copying all subscription lines and replacing any date variables in the descriptions.
2. Sets the invoice date to today and the due date from the administration's payment term.
3. Advances the subscription's **next invoice date** by one interval, so the following period is queued automatically.

Whether the invoice is sent or saved as a draft depends on **Auto-send**:

- **Auto-send on** — if your account has sufficient credits, the invoice gets a real invoice number, is emailed to the recipients you selected, and credits are deducted.
- **Auto-send off** — the invoice is created as a **draft** with a concept number for you to send yourself.

<Note>
If today is a day excluded from invoicing for the client, auto-send is skipped and the invoice is created as a draft instead. The same happens if your account is short on credits.
</Note>
