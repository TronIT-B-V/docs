---
title: "Product categories"
description: "Group your products with categories to keep a large catalogue organised and filter quickly when adding lines to invoices and quotes."
---

Categories are a simple way to organise your product catalogue. As your product list grows, categories let you group related items — for example *Consultancy*, *Software licences*, or *Travel costs* — and filter the products list down to what you need.

Categories are shared across your whole organisation, so every administration uses the same set.

## Viewing categories

Go to **Producten → Categorieën** (Products → Categories) to see the **Productcategorieën** list. For each category you can see its name, description, and whether it is active. Use the search box and status filter to find a category quickly.

## Creating a category

You can create a category in two places.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="From the categories list">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the form">
        On the **Productcategorieën** list, click **New category**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Fill in the details">
        Enter a **name** (required) and an optional **description**. New categories are active by default.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save">
        Save the category. It is immediately available in the category selector on the product form.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="While editing a product">
    The quick category form lets you create a category without leaving the product form. When the category you need does not exist yet, add it inline — enter a name, save, and the new category is selected for the product straight away.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Category names must be unique within your organisation. If you enter a name that already exists, the form asks you to choose another.
</Note>

## Editing and deactivating a category

Open a category from the list to edit its name or description, or to change its active status.

Deactivating a category hides it from the category selector on the product form, but it does **not** affect products already assigned to it — those products keep their category. Reactivate the category at any time to make it selectable again.

<Tip>
  Deactivate categories you no longer use instead of deleting them. Existing products stay organised and you can bring the category back later.
</Tip>

## Assigning products to a category

A product's category is set on the product form, under the **Category** field.

- Pick an existing category from the dropdown, or create one inline with the quick form.
- A product can belong to one category at a time.
- The category field is optional — products without a category still work normally on invoices and quotes.

To re-categorise a product, open it and choose a different category.

## Deleting a category

You can delete a category from the categories list, but only when **no products are assigned to it**. If a category still has products, TRONVoice blocks the deletion and tells you how many products are linked.

<Warning>
  To delete a category in use, first move its products to another category (or clear their category), then delete it.
</Warning>

## Filtering by category

Once your products are categorised, you can filter the products list by category to find the right item faster — useful when adding lines to an invoice or quote from a large catalogue.
