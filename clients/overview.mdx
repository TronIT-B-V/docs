The clients section is your central address book. Every invoice, subscription, and quote you create is linked to a client record, so keeping your client data accurate and complete saves time across the whole platform.

## Adding a client

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the clients list">
    Go to **Clients** in the main navigation and click **New client**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search the KvK register (optional)">
    Use the KvK search at the top of the form to find a Dutch company by name or registration number. Selecting a result fills in the company name, KvK number, and address automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the client details">
    Complete at minimum the **Company name**. All other fields are optional but recommended:

    | Field | Notes |
    |---|---|
    | Company name | Required. Also used for individual clients. |
    | KvK number | Dutch Chamber of Commerce registration number. |
    | VAT number | Validated in real time via the EU VIES service. |
    | Address | Street, postcode, city, country. |
    | Contact person | Name of your primary contact. |
    | Email address | Used for sending invoices. Supports multiple addresses separated by commas or new lines. |
    | Phone | Optional contact number. |
    | Website | Used to automatically fetch the company logo. |
    | IBAN | Bank account number, relevant for subcontractors. |
    | Internal reference | Your own code or ID for this client (e.g. a CRM reference). |
    | Document language | Language used for invoices and emails sent to this client. |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the client">
    Click **Create client**. The client record opens immediately so you can add notes, tasks, or files.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## KvK lookup

The KvK search lets you find any registered Dutch company directly from the client form. Type a company name or KvK number and select the correct result — the form fields for company name, registration number, address, city, and postcode are filled in automatically.

<Tip>
  The KvK lookup works best with official trade names. If the search returns no results, enter the details manually.
</Tip>

## VAT number validation

When you enter a VAT (BTW) number for an EU client, TRONVoice checks it in real time against the **EU VIES database**. A green indicator confirms the number is valid and registered.

VAT settings are also detected automatically based on the client's country:

- **Same country as your administration** — standard VAT rates apply.
- **Another EU country** — VAT is automatically set to *reversed* (BTW verlegd), as required for intra-community supplies.
- **Outside the EU** — VAT is automatically set to *exempt* (BTW vrijgesteld), for export deliveries.

You can override these settings manually on the client form.

## Company logo

When you save a client with a website or email address, TRONVoice automatically fetches the company logo from the website. The logo appears on the client record and can be used on invoices.

The logo is refreshed automatically whenever the website or email address changes.

## Client dossier, notes, and tasks

Each client has three built-in tools for managing ongoing information:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Dossier" icon="folder">
    Attach files directly to a client record — contracts, signed agreements, or any other documents you want to keep together.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Notes" icon="sticky-note">
    Add internal notes to a client. Notes are only visible to your team and never appear on invoices or emails.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tasks" icon="list-checks">
    Create to-do items linked to a client so follow-up actions stay connected to the right record.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Importing clients from CSV or Excel

If you have an existing client list, you can import it in bulk instead of adding clients one by one.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the import page">
    From the clients list, click **Import** and download the example template to see the expected column format.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare your file">
    Fill in your data using the template. Required column: **bedrijfsnaam** (company name). All other columns are optional.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload and confirm">
    Upload your CSV or Excel file. TRONVoice shows a preview of the rows it will import. Confirm to complete the import.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Duplicate records are not automatically detected during import. Check your file for duplicate company names or KvK numbers before uploading.
</Note>

## Clients across multiple administrations

If your account has more than one administration, each client belongs to a specific administration. You can view all clients across all administrations from the **All clients** overview in the main navigation.

When creating a client from the all-clients view, you select which administration the client belongs to.

## Mandate management

Clients can have a SEPA direct debit mandate linked to their record, enabling automatic collection of invoice amounts. See [SEPA direct debit](/payments/sepa-direct-debit) for setup instructions.
