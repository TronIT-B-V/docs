---
title: "Get started with TRONVoice"
description: "Create your TRONVoice account, enable mandatory 2FA, configure your administration, add a client, and send your first invoice in minutes."
---

This guide walks you through everything you need to do to go from a new account to sending your first invoice with TRONVoice. Follow the steps in order — each one unlocks the next.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [tronvoice.eu](https://tronvoice.eu) and click **Account aanmaken**.

    You can sign up with your email address and a password, or use one of the social login options:

    - **Google**
    - **Microsoft**
    - **Apple**

    During registration, choose how many administrations you need. Your first 2 are included for free. If you manage multiple companies or client books, you can add extra administrations at €3.00 per month each — and you'll receive a 10% discount on invoice costs.

    New accounts receive **€2.50 in free credits** to get you started.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)">
    After registration, TRONVoice takes you straight to the 2FA setup screen. Two-factor authentication is required before you can access the dashboard.

    To set up 2FA, you need an authenticator app on your phone. Any of these work:

    - Google Authenticator
    - Microsoft Authenticator
    - Authy

    **How to set it up:**

    1. Enter your password to confirm your identity.
    2. Open your authenticator app and scan the QR code shown on screen.
    3. Enter the 6-digit code from your app to confirm it's working.
    4. Save the recovery codes that appear — store them somewhere safe. If you ever lose access to your phone, these codes let you get back in.

    <Warning>
      Do not skip saving your recovery codes. If you lose your phone and don't have the recovery codes, you will not be able to access your account.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your organisation and administration">
    Once 2FA is active, TRONVoice walks you through setting up your company details.

    Fill in:
    - **Bedrijfsnaam** — your company name
    - **KvK-nummer** — your Chamber of Commerce registration number
    - **BTW-nummer** — your VAT number (e.g. `NL123456789B01`)
    - **Adres, postcode, plaats** — your business address
    - **IBAN** — your business bank account number (shown on every invoice)

    <Tip>
      Use the KvK search field to find your company by name or KvK number. TRONVoice fills in most fields automatically from the Chamber of Commerce registry.
    </Tip>

    After saving your company details, your first **administratie** is created. An administratie is a separate set of books — it has its own invoice series, branding, and client list. If you run more than one company, you can add additional administrations later from your account settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your first client">
    From the dashboard, go to **Klanten** and click **Nieuwe klant**.

    Enter your client's name, email address, and any other relevant details. Once saved, the client is available to select when creating invoices.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create and send your first invoice">
    Go to **Facturen** and click **Nieuwe factuur**.

    1. Select the client you just created.
    2. Add one or more invoice lines — description, quantity, and price.
    3. TRONVoice calculates BTW automatically based on your default rate.
    4. Preview the PDF, then click **Versturen** to send the invoice by email.

    Each sent invoice costs **€0.65** in credits, deducted from your credit balance.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Top up your credits">
    Your credits are shown in the top bar of the dashboard. When your balance runs low, go to **Credits** and choose an amount to top up.

    You can also enable **auto top-up**: TRONVoice automatically charges your payment method when your balance falls below a threshold you set (default: €5.00), so you're never blocked from sending.

    <Tip>
      TRONVoice has a monthly cap of **€16.25**. Once you've spent that in a calendar month, all further invoices and quotes are free until the month resets. If you send invoices frequently, you'll often hit the cap quickly — making the effective cost per invoice much lower.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
